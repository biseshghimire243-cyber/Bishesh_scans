🚀 QR Studio

QR Studio is a modern QR Code Management System that allows users to generate, scan and manage QR Codes through a simple dashboard.


✨ Features

  • ✅ QR Code Generator
  • ✅ QR Code Scanner
  • ✅ QR History
  • ✅ Business Card QR
  • ✅ Download QR Code
  • ✅ Responsive Dashboard
